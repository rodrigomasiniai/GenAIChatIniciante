GenAI, short for Generative Artificial Intelligence, refers to AI systems that have the capability to create or generate new content, such as images, text, music, or even entire pieces of code. These systems learn patterns and structures from existing data and then use that knowledge to produce novel outputs. Generative AI has applications in various fields, including art, design, gaming, and content creation. It is used for tasks like image synthesis, text generation, style transfer, and more. GenAI systems are continuously evolving and have the potential to revolutionize how we create and interact with digital content.
